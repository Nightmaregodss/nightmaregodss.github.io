About me

In 2021, I started my adventure into the field of software development at the youthful age of 13. I began by utilizing discord.js to create Discord bots, which helped me quickly become proficient in JavaScript. Even though I changed my priorities at the beginning of 2022, I moved into the hosting industry with ease and became an another normal developer for many hosting platforms. I started Fex, a growing developer community then ditched it in 2023. As a result of my varied skills and unwavering commitment to innovation, I am the head development for Holaclient-E, a platform that is an alternative to Pterodactyl. My goal with Holaclient-E is to revolutionize the hosting industry by providing improved features, smooth user interface and a client integrated in panel. I'm dedicated to bringing about positive change and making a long-lasting impression on the development landscape as I keep pushing the envelope of what's possible in the technology business. My experience to date bears witness to my commitment, flexibility, and unwavering desire to make significant contributions to the field of technology.
